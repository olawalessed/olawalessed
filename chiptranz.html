Chiptranz makes savings easy 

Chiptranz is changing how you save your money, as it redesigns platform

Overview

Chiptranz is a platform that offers savings and investment with profitable interests to the users. It offers robust functionality that makes saving toward goals and future plans a simple process for the users.
The backend functionality of this product is working perfectly and the engineering team is working tirelessly to improve it.
The aim of this project was to redesign the interface for both the webapp and the mobile responsive views. 

Roles

Product Designer (Contract)
Activities
User Interface
User Experience
Prototyping & Testing

Problem

Poor interface increases churn rate due to competitor’s products offering more visually designed interfaces.
Savings experience for the user is not interactive and engaging thereby causing a barrier to user successfully using the platform

User Research

The following were considered during the research phase 
The product documentation was studied to check for important informations that could influence the user experience
Moodboarding ideas from around the internet to see likely patterns that converts
Studying the existing savings experience and how it can be optimized
Peoples complaints on existing apps on the playstore

Insights

Upon getting to look into the product and understanding people’s behaviour, I studied existing solutions, to analyze how the product can be optimized and design a better simple and engaging experience 

Ideation

Initializing, by selecting the key functionalities and converting them into a more simple experience. Changing the interfaces elements and improving on the visual elements starting from the Brand identity, brand Colors and also the product elements. After the first meeting with the product owner and manager, I was given the go ahead to convert all the existing screens into a more simple and functional interface. 

Redesign Process

I redesigned the following major part of the product and the tools used


The Brand Identity     - Adobe Illustrator

User Interface and Experience    - Figma & InVision


All the existing pages and functionalities with an improvement to the user experience were made.

Feedbacks

Ability to keep the stakeholders on the project in the know can save a lot of ups and downs. Quick decisions and suggestions can be made. Also this makes work faster, and not putting the designer in a silo.
