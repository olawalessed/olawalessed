Setmyshop

On-demand e-commerce and website design service for small and medium business owners.

Introduction

E-commerce and Business Website has seen a greater increase over the years. But, there are still some concerns and certain painpoints when it comes building a website for one's business or setting up a shop online.

Some of this problems include;

- Low level knowledge in design and development of a website or online store setup.
- Lack of adequate management for each website thereby leading to loss of details or access
- High increase in online website and online store attack

Setmyshop as a Website-As-a-Service WaaS will be building a mobile platform for small and medium business owners to manage their online presence from the palm of their finger.


My Role on this project

I worked on this project as a product designer making decisions on the user interface, user experience, user interaction, branding and the business side-effect of building this product.

I do not have the full access to share some details in full, but I will try based on the limit of access i posses.


How do we start

According to research made with the team member at Setmyshop, more than 400,000 businesses opens in a year across Africa, thereby creating a huge target users for this solution.

Also according to a google doc result, it was vividly discovered that this solution is going to be possible but, there is a need to sensitize business owners on what they can get and waht there expectations are to be.


Who are the Target Users?

We created a persona of a group of three (3). Details can't be revealed more than this extent.


Ideation

Using Invision Freehand, The product was sketched after creating a moodboard of possible existing solutions. This made the whole ideation a little more easier to achieve.

It was simple to make decisions with the freehand project feature. Testing was done swiftly and quickly, which also made it more faster to really get a feedback towards the ideation direction.


Prototyping

I used Invision Studio to create a more high quality version of the idea we had voted for based on the feedbacks gotten from the sketches. Normally, we could have gone to do a wireframe for this product, but, we thought that since we already can use Invision Freehand to get a quick feedback, and also knowing that this was the same result we would get from the wireframing process, then, we decided to just make the Sketches as our wireframe already.


Branding

Setmyshop is a strong brand name that already speaks for itself, this made the brand image already pointed us in a direction to go. We voted for Simplicity and Class. But, friendly, because this product will be interfacing with alots of businesses and it could use a little form of customer friendly tone. But, as a service based product, it will have a feeling that makes users thirsty for the brand as a whole. It's services, customer experience and pricing.

What Next? 

Setmyshop will create a solution that empowers youths and other classes to put their various businesses online with no headache. I personally look forward to when they finally launch this product. I believe I have been able to create a solution that impact people's life and help them do things easily.
