Bitshore

Savings - Investment - Mobile Banking

The Power of Smart Banking

Technology has taken banking to a whole new level. mobile banking is changing the rule of the game, it is now easy to save, invest and bank on the mobile app all at once.

Bitshore is a savings and investment platform and a Mobile Banking application that is built for Africans and Africans in Diaspora.
It focuses on giving users the value they could get with their money on different service offerings that span through all classes of users.

I worked on this project as a UI/UX designer, building the product usage and experience
